Personal Information

Interest in IT

I have always had an interest in IT since I was young trying to figure out how my computer worked, honestly it’s a bit stupid it took me this long to figure out I should study and make a career out of that interest. My background in IT is very minimal, mostly base level computer building ect. I’m a video editor and studio operator by day for a news broadcaster so I deal with complex IT systems everyday but mostly just press buttons and pray it already works. News broadcast as it stands is in my opinion dying and not going to get me anywhere I want to go so I enrolled in order to change up my career. Cloud computing interests me currently as it is in use within our regional news studio at work and while the little cloud computing we use works for what we need I believe it could facilitate a lot more for us and others.

Why I chose RMIT

I chose to study with RMIT through OUA as I was able to fit my studies around my full time work hours. I also believed RMIT would be the best university for me to acquire the knowledge and skills for me to change careers in the future.

What I expect to Learn

Aside from learning programming, data systems, computing, and just listening off the subjects I will be taking throughout this degree, I expect to gain a better understanding of many of the different faculties that are encompassed by I.T. I hope to learn and understand where I will fit into the world of I.T in the near future what my interests will be and what areas I will further explore as a career in my future.
